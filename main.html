Summary

*Inception* is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets through dreams. Given a chance to have his criminal history erased, he takes on the task of planting an idea in someone's mind — a process called inception. As the team journeys deeper into layered dreams, reality blurs with imagination.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
