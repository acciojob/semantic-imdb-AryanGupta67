Summary

"Inception" is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story revolves around Dom Cobb, a thief who enters people’s dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for planting an idea into someone's mind — a process known as inception.

Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
